Boolean Attributes

There are Boolean attributes in HTML, and there are about two dozen of them—25, to be precise, unless I err somewhere. Here’s a quick rundown of what makes for a Boolean attribute, and what Boolean attributes there are in current HTML.The presence of a boolean attribute on an element represents the “true” value, and the absence of the attribute represents the “false” value.

Here some boolean attributes are listed below:-

  1. controls
  2. readonly
  3. required
  4. allowfullscreen
  5. autoplay
  6. autofocus
  7. checked
  8. default
  9. disable
  10. hidden
  11. muted
  12. selected etc.
